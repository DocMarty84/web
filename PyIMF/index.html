9GAG RSS Feed

Not so long ago, 9GAG was providing RSS feeds. The functionality was removed, probably to force the users to visit the website or to download the app. Since I didn't want to do any of those, I started to build my own RSS feeds from their content. The project is now mature enough, so I decided to make it available for free to everyone. Similar projects already exist, but they didn't meet my requirements.

You will find below all feeds you can get at 9GAG, from the usual "Hot", "Trending" or "Fresh" feeds to the category-specific feeds. Feeds are updated every 2 minutes, and the last 200 posts are made accessible. Once again, it is a free service, and no advertisement is inserted at any point.

Add the feeds you want in your favorite feed reader, and you are good to go!
